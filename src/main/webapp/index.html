About

The journey of Sameer Travels began in the year 2000. We were set out with one car but with a drive to succeed in the race.

It was a challenge to meet the requirements of the customers with just one car at the disposal. But the sincerity to serve did not go unnoticed and soon the company was associated with corporate clients.

Tie-ups with some esteemed multinational & large and small national companies followed and Sameer Travels became a force to reckon with on the road.

The company’s cars are now hired on call as well as yearly contract basis. What put it ahead in the fiercely competitive marketplace was uncompromising hard work, punctuality, safety, periodic renewal of fleet and excellent friendly back office support.

In 2002, the company rolled out air-conditioned cars for Chhindwada – Nagpur transfer. The highlight of the service was residence pickup and drop within Chhindwada City and adjoining areas.

The journey so far has taken the company places with a fleet of 75 cabs. Despite the growth, the motto still remains delighting the customers on their journeys, no matter how long or short that be.
